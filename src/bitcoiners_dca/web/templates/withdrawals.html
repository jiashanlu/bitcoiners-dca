Auto-withdraw

Once your exchange BTC balance crosses the threshold, the bot sweeps it to a hardcoded destination address. Exchange-as-custodian risk → near-zero.

Destination

Hardcode YOUR hardware-wallet address. The bot has no UI to change it later — that's the point. Generate the address on your hardware wallet, verify on-device, paste here.

Re-verify on your hardware wallet before saving. Once enabled the bot withdraws automatically.
Withdrawals fire when exchange BTC balance ≥ this. Typical: 0.001-0.01 BTC. Lower threshold = more withdrawals = more on-chain fees.

Safety

  • Use a fresh address from your hardware wallet, not a previously-used one.
  • Trade-only API key is enough — the exchange's withdraw whitelist authorizes the address, not the bot.
  • Set the exchange's withdraw address whitelist to ONLY this address before turning auto-withdraw on. Even if the bot is compromised, the most an attacker can do is trigger your already-whitelisted withdraw.
  • For Lightning withdrawals (Pro tier, OKX only): leave the address field as a BTC on-chain address and use the CLI bitcoiners-dca withdraw lnbc1… path for one-shot Lightning invoices.
